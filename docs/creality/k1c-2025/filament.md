## Load
https://wiki.creality.com/en/k1-flagship-series/k1c/load-unload-replace-filament#h-2-semi-automatic-filament-loading-recommended-method

1. Open the top
2. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever to **UNLOCKED**
3. Clip the filament end at a 45 degree angle
4. Feed the filament into the feeder tube on the back until it can't be pushed any further.
5. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever back to **LOCKED**
6. Settings -> Filament
7. Push the "Extrude" button
8. Should see filament being extruded from the nozzle; wait for it to finish
9. If nothing extruded, push filament up again to ensure it's fed properly
    - Push the "Extrude" button again


## Unload
https://wiki.creality.com/en/k1-flagship-series/k1c/load-unload-replace-filament#h-3-automatic-filament-retraction

1. Settings -> Filament
2. Push the "Retract" button
3. Wait for the screen to say the retraction is complete
4. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever to **UNLOCKED**
5. Pull the filament out of the feeder tube on the back.
6. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever back to **LOCKED**


## Clean Teflon Filament Tube
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5M8DOHWg7cg

1. Settings -> Filament -> Retract
2. Wait to finish retraction
3. Open the top
4. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever to **UNLOCKED**
5. Push down on the white ring around the teflon tube & pull up on the tube to remove the tube & filament from the extruder
6. Use the cleaning pin to clean nozzle if necessary
7. Clean out the teflon tube of any broken filament or debris
8. Load new filament into the tube from the back (like normal)
9. Feed until it reaches the end of the tube near the extruder
10. Extend the filament a little bit more
11. Feed to filament into the extruder as far as it will go (don't force it)
12. Push the teflon tube back into extruder into the white ring until it locks into place
13. Flip the Extruder Lock Lever to **LOCKED**
14. Settings -> Filament -> Extrude
15. Wait for the extrustion to finish
